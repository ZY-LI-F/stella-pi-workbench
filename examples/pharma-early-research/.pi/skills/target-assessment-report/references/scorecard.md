# Target assessment scorecard

Score 0–5 for each dimension, multiply by its weight, then divide by 5. Show each subtotal.

| Dimension | Weight | Minimum evidence expected |
|---|---:|---|
| Human causal/genetic validation | 20 | Human variant, perturbation, or intervention evidence tied to the indication |
| Disease-mechanism coherence | 15 | Directional mechanism connecting target modulation to disease biology |
| Translational biomarkers | 15 | Measurable target engagement and downstream pharmacodynamic chain |
| Druggability and selectivity | 15 | Relevant binding/functional assays and a credible modality path |
| Safety and therapeutic window | 15 | On-target physiology, tissue distribution, chronic-use and infection/immune risks |
| Competitive differentiation | 10 | Current clinical assets, failures, whitespace, and a testable differentiation claim |
| Indication and trial feasibility | 10 | Population, endpoint, duration, comparator, and enrichment strategy |

Decision bands: 75–100 Go; 60–74 Conditional Go; 45–59 Hold; below 45 No-Go. A score cannot exceed 74 when either human causal/genetic validation or safety/therapeutic window is below 2/5. Any unresolved credible fatal safety signal forces Hold until resolved.
