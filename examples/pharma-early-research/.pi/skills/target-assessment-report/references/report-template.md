# Report template

1. Decision header: target, modality, indication, snapshot date, owner, recommendation, total score.
2. Executive decision: three decision-driving positives, three risks, and the exact boundary of the recommendation.
3. Target identity and mechanism.
4. Human validation and indication evidence.
5. Expression, tractability, selectivity, and safety.
6. Translational biomarker chain.
7. Clinical competitor table with NCT IDs and status dates.
8. Weighted scorecard with evidence rationale.
9. Critical uncertainties, contradictory evidence, and falsifiers.
10. Ninety-day evidence plan with experiment, readout, decision threshold, owner role, and dependency.
11. Source ledger: claim, source URL/path, retrieval date, evidence class, limitation.
