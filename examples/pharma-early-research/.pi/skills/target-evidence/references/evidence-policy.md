# Evidence policy

Use source priority in this order: primary human study or official database record, primary non-human study, official sponsor disclosure, peer-reviewed synthesis, then secondary reporting. Label sponsor claims as sponsor-reported.

Open Targets association scores combine heterogeneous evidence and rank target-disease support; they are not causal effect sizes. Human Protein Atlas RNA detection does not prove functional protein abundance. ChEMBL activity rows can mix organisms, assay formats, endpoints, and confidence; do not pool potency without harmonizing those fields.

For every major conclusion record: claim, source, entity identifier, retrieval date, evidence class, direction, limitation, and whether an independent source corroborates it.
