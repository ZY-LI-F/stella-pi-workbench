# Clinical source policy

For recruitment and trial status, prefer the latest registry record and record its update date. A sponsor release can explain a strategy or disclose top-line results, but it does not override a conflicting registry status without an explicit reconciliation note.

Separate these facts: development phase, trial phase, overall trial status, results posted, sponsor-reported result, and sponsor-reported next milestone. “Completed” does not mean positive; “recruiting” does not mean dosed; “planned” does not mean initiated.

When names or ownership changed, retain the historical alias and identify the current registry sponsor. Never merge two assets solely because both target the same protein.
